My name is Anthony Boarman and I am a currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Instructional Technology at The University of Akron. I work at St. Vincent-St. Mary High School in Akron, Ohio where I teach U.S. History, Economics & Personal Finance, Current & Future Issues, and next year I will be adding a Local/Akron History class. I am the head baseball coach and an assistant football coach. I also run our school's website, so I have gained some valuable knowledge in HTML, CSS, and graphic design.

In my free time, which can be limited, I enjoy exploring our parks in Northeast Ohio and have recently taken up photography as a hobby by learning how to use a DSLR camera. It has been a fun, new challenge for me and has helped to give me a release away from work.

In 2015, I married my wife Emily. We live in West Akron with our dog Ava. Emily is a school counselor at St. Vincent-St. Mary, so we both have a deep passion for the Fighting Irish! We both love our local parks and plan to visit a new National Park or two each year. Last year we visited Yosemite and this summer we are going to Glacier.

I look forward to learning more about hypermedia and multimedia this semester!
